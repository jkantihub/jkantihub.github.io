And now Google energy

General

The hopes are high.The world is flat.

Here Google.org presents Google’s view on climate change.

The proposal is to make the following things possible on your list of energy consumptions.

  1. Use energy efficient products. This clearly translates to a competitive marketplace where you will find new and innovative products which consume less and less electricity.Look at your mobile charger(too small an example).How much of energy does it consume.Is it required that you put the charger plugged in all the time. Look at the number of other equipment lights, fans , coolers , AC , TV , Fridge and the other things. Try to use as efficient energy product as possible.

  2. Measuring your energy consumption What we do today is pay for the energy consumed.The day the electricity bill arrives we look oh, that is the amount of energy we used.The thought should change.We should start measuring energy real time.Whats the current consumption.What if a particular equipment if changed across the board.

  3. Sources of energy The main source of electricity today is the Electrical Grid.We need to find ways to tap other sources of energy,cleaner energy which can be reused.Think of something which can be recycled.Maybe a battery which could be charged 10000 times.Maybe a cradle and shaft which generates electricity when we cycle,run or walk.Small small innovations but innovations which are smart.Which can reduce the burden on Mother earth and make our living more cleaner and efficient.

There is a lot more to do , to research and to make things happen.
