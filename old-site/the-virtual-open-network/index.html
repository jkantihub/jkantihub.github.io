The Virtual Open Network

Random

The recent case of allowing access on open wifi routers got a person in the police network[1].His only problem was that he knowingly/unknowingly left his wifi router open for access.At one end of the side you can easily notice the innocence in the case,but if you look at it from the other angle its called a security hole.Anybody with sufficient malafide intent could simply play with this setup and cause problems for the person opening up his network.

There is this interesting preposition where EFF is encouraging you to share your wifi network[2].The request puts it in perspective as to how it would be a community good if we have had unconditional access to the Internet as you would for your basic rights.In Finland there is a legislation[3] which makes access to Internet a right for every citizen.The use of the Internet is not so common in third world countries but a survey conducted recently concludes that Internet access helps people increase their knowledge, makes them more open to communication and is a public good.<!– more –>

What if we could create a network which would allow anyone access to Internet? What if it helped you keep the anonymity on the Internet intact?Imagine situations where you are on the road and need Internet access.You are stuck in a place where there is no standard telecom network.Hilly regions where you are on a trekking trip and find yourself disconnected[4].It would be a nice or must have situation where you want Internet access.To solve these issues we could put together a model which keeps the Internet open and accessible for the people.

The virtual open network is nothing but the intent to keep the Internet free for access.To make it omnipresent so that it is available anywhere and everywhere.The need for this is enormous , the technical challenge huge , but is it needed? yes it is.The Internet should be kept open.It should not be locked down by commercial interests and government control.It is part of your digital freedom.Make the Internet just about one more public utility.

An example of such a public network is not difficult to find.The electric network is one such example.Imagine a situation where your electricity provider makes it just about impossible for you to get electricity.The cost rise of electricity is just about impossible.Would you look for a way out? Would you opt for a different electricity provider? Yes , if you had that option.

The need of the day is to ensure we keep the Internet open.We need to continuously thrive for it.We need to keep finding ways to leave it for easy accessibility.We need to keep it anonymous.We need to keep it free.We need to keep it open.
