Taare Zameen Par

Today i went to watch the movie Taare Zameen Par ( TZP ).
Its a smart story of aspirations.To start with , i have not seen such a colourful movie in recent times.
The work done on the graphics by Tata Elxsi is good too.

When a small kid having dyslexia is forced to go through the rigorous
schedule of daily schooling he tends to rebel.Most of the times he
expresses his feelings in ways that his parents do not understand at first.
He is packed off to a boarding school to get some discipline and that is where
he gets to meet his wonderful arts teacher.
To admit it frankly that there is a problem in the way schooling is done
these days in most parts of our country is truly glorified.
The underlying statement is true , we are creating horses to run.
Run like anything , 95 , 96 , 97 and the points together makes the kids run and run and run.

What i liked most about this movie is the wonderful performance by the kid(Darsheel) in the movie.
The remarks by other teachers in the end makes things better for all of the other kids.
The film exemplifies the important points and brings home the real winners.
Winners of life.

Age really shows up on Aamir’s face but ultimately his performance rocks.
With this being his own production , it had to be a very good attempt.
Indeed it wins by all accounts.
